Accessibility

We are committed to ensuring an inclusive and accessible digital experience for all users.

Accessibility Statement

Vitolo is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, including people with disabilities. We continuously work to improve the user experience for everyone and apply relevant accessibility standards to achieve this goal.

Accessibility is not a one-time effort, but an ongoing process. We are dedicated to regularly monitoring, updating, and improving our website to make it more accessible, inclusive, and user-friendly.

Our Commitment

We believe that every person has the right to access digital content with dignity, equality, comfort, and independence. Our website is designed to support a wide range of users, including those who rely on assistive technologies.

Accessibility Features

  • ✔ Text size adjustment
  • ✔ High contrast mode
  • ✔ Highlighting links
  • ✔ Dyslexia-friendly font
  • ✔ Increased spacing and line height
  • ✔ Ability to pause animations
  • ✔ Screen reader support
  • ✔ Keyboard navigation improvements

Standards & Guidelines

We aim to follow best practices and standards defined by the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG), developed by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C).

While we strive to meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards, accessibility is an ongoing effort, and we continue to improve our website over time.

Continuous Improvement

We regularly review our website using accessibility tools and manual testing to identify and fix potential issues. Our goal is to ensure that all users can access our content without barriers.
